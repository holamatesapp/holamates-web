How to play

Sum 10

Numbers appear on the screen. The first one is blue.
Tap the number that adds up with it to make 10.

Depending on the level, numbers appear faster and faster.

If you get it right, the number disappears.

If you fail, another number appears as a penalty.

⏱️ The game ends after 60 seconds or when there are 10 numbers on screen.

Other games

Operations appear on the screen. The first one (blue) is the active one. Enter the result.

Depending on the level, they appear faster.

If you get it right, it disappears.

If you fail, another one appears as a penalty.

⏱️ 60 seconds or 10 operations.

💪 Try to improve your score in every game.
