What is Suma10?

Suma10 is more than just a game. It’s real mental math training. Students learn to instantly recognise number combinations that make 10. This improves their mental agility and helps them move beyond counting on their fingers.

Real number fluency

  • Understanding the base-10 system
  • Breaking numbers apart (number decomposition)
  • Developing mental calculation strategies

Mental math games platform for elementary students

HolaMates is a mental math games platform designed for elementary students to improve math skills in a fun and engaging way. Children practice addition, subtraction, multiplication and division through interactive math games that build strong mental calculation skills without frustration.

Perfect for classroom and home use, HolaMates helps students develop mental agility, focus and confidence in math. It is an ideal resource for teachers and parents looking for effective and motivating math practice for elementary education.
